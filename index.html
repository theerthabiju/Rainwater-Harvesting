About Us

Rainwater Harvesting is a forward-thinking environmental solutions company dedicated to addressing India’s growing water crisis through innovation, sustainability, and technology. Our mission is to transform how communities manage, store, and reuse rainwater — turning every drop into a valuable resource for the future. At Rainwater Harvesting, we believe that solving water scarcity begins with smarter infrastructure. That’s why we’ve developed our breakthrough innovation — the Smart Drainage System (SDS). This patented 4-in-1 modular system is designed to divert, drain, store, and reuse rainwater efficiently. Unlike traditional concrete pipes or tanks, SDS offers a lightweight, eco-friendly, and cost-effective solution that prevents urban flooding while conserving water for sustainable use.
Backed by international patents and years of dedicated research, SDS represents a major leap forward in stormwater management. Its modular, recyclable design makes it easy to install, transport, and adapt for diverse applications — from roadworks and construction sites to residential and commercial projects. Through innovation like SDS, Rainwater Harvesting is building a cleaner, greener, and more water-secure India — where technology and nature work hand in hand to protect our planet’s most vital resource.
